Test Case Title: Add all 6 products to cart and then remove them
Test Case Objective: Verify adding all 6 products to the cart and then removing them

Precondition:
- User is logged in
- User is on the inventory page (https://www.saucedemo.com/inventory.html)

Steps:

1. Navigate to the login page (https://www.saucedemo.com/) and log in
   Data: Username: standard_user, Password: secret_sauce
   Expected result: User is logged in, inventory page is displayed

2. Click the "Add to cart" button for each of the 6 products
   Expected result: All 6 products are added to the cart, cart icon shows "6"

3. Click on the cart icon
   Expected result: Cart page is opened

4. Click the "Remove" button next to each of the 6 products
   Expected result: All 6 products are removed from the cart, cart icon disappears or shows "0"

5. Verify the cart is empty
   Expected result: Cart is empty, no products are displayed in the cart page
